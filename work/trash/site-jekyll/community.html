United We Stand

Hook up with other users and broaden the scope of your usage. Ruby Facets has a moto, "All Base Common".

Mailing List

Facets has a dedicated mailing list hosted via Rubyforge.org, used by developers and end-users alike. You can subscribe to it directly, or subscribe via the Google Groups archive of the list.

http://rubyforge.org
http://groups.google.com/group/facets-universal/topics?hl=en

Wiki Wiki

The Wiki is a place for end-users to create their own documentation. Ultimately, it is hoped, this documentaiton will make a great source for sharing real-world use cases. However, a full Wiki solution isn't in place yetand we are simply using Google Pages, provided along side the Google mailing list interface.

The pages are here: http://groups.google.com/group/facets-universal/web?hl=en

Plans are in the works to provided a dedicated Wiki for Facets via GitHub. Stay tuned.
